The estimate feature solicits the knowledge of others to narrow down the range of numbers a true value is believed lie within.

Some examples:

  • How many coins are in a jar?
  • How much money earned in sales next month?
  • How often will a new piece of hardware fail?

Panelists will submit their minimum and maximum values, creating a range of values they believe the answer to lie within.

Panelists are expected to be calibrated and trained to guess with 90% certainty.
