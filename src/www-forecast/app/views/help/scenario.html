The scenario feature solicits the knowledge of others to discover certainty associated with future outcomes.

Some examples:

  • Will it rain or shine tomorrow between 3pm-5pm?
  • Will an event happen several times, or not at all?
  • Will a certain candidate win an election?
  • Will a disaster take place?

Panelists will submit probabilities, which will give insight into the groups certainty about the future.

Panelists are expected to be calibrated and trained to think probabilistically.
